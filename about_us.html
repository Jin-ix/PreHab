Welcome to Prehab, a smart injury risk prediction and recovery assistant for footballers. We're closing the gap between expensive elite technology and the teams that need it most, making top-tier injury prevention accessible to all.

The Problem We're Solving

Frequent training, matches, and poor recovery make footballers highly injury-prone. Elite clubs use expensive wearables to manage this, but most teams simply can't afford them. This creates a huge gap in player care, leaving athletes at risk and coaches without the data needed for safe training decisions. While tools for tactical analysis exist, they are not built specifically for injury prediction.

Our Solution: The Prehab Advantage

Prehab is a machine learning-powered web app that forecasts injury risk without expensive hardware. We provide an intelligence dashboard that uses player data to predict injuries and offer tailored training and recovery recommendations.

Affordability & Accessibility

We provide a low-cost system for amateur and semi-pro teams, built on open-source tools like Python and Streamlit to ensure it remains accessible.

Integrated Intelligence

Our platform unifies data analysis, risk prediction, and personalized guidance into a single, seamless application.

Personalized Guidance

Our virtual assistant provides tailored recovery and training advice, moving beyond generic rehab plans.

Our Mission & Objectives

Our primary objective is to develop a machine learning-powered web app that forecasts football injury risk, visualizes player workload trends, and offers personalized recovery advice through a virtual assistant.

  • Simulate Data: To simulate realistic player workload data that reflects typical training, recovery, and match patterns.
  • Classify Risk: To classify injury risk levels (Low, Moderate, High, Very High) using machine learning.
  • Build a Dashboard: To build a dashboard for coaches and players to track individual performance and injury trends over time.
  • Enable Data Handling: To enable data upload and export features (e.g., CSV import, PDF report generation) for ease of use.

The Technology Behind Prehab

Our system is built on a foundation of robust and proven technologies to ensure accuracy and reliability for coaches and players.

  • Core Algorithm: We use a Random Forest Classifier. It works very well with structured data, handles many input features, and is robust to noise. It also tells you which features are most important.
  • System Design: The platform allows coaching staff to upload player data, which is processed by our model to predict risk and generate downloadable reports.
  • Data Sources: Our model is informed by simulated wearable-style datasets and public data from sources like the FIFA Football Dataset and NCAA's Sports Injury Surveillance System.
